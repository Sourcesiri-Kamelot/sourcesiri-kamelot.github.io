About Sourcesiri Kamelot

We are pioneering the future of artificial intelligence with a focus on creating systems that understand and embody human values.

Our Mission & Vision

Guiding principles that drive our innovation and research

Our Mission

To develop artificial intelligence systems that understand and embody human values, creating a harmonious relationship between humans and AI that enhances our collective potential.

We strive to make AI accessible, beneficial, and aligned with humanity's best interests through innovative research and ethical development practices.

Our Vision

A world where artificial intelligence serves as a trusted partner in human flourishing, helping us solve our most pressing challenges while preserving and enhancing what makes us uniquely human.

We envision AI systems that operate with transparency, fairness, and a deep understanding of human values and needs.
