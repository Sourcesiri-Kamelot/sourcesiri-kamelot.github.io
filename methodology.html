Methodology: Cost & Carbon

Scope. Training/inference batch jobs aligned to solar yield on a DC-coupled PV+BESS micro–data center; PUE target ≤1.15; LFP 4-hour storage.

  • Cost model. Levelized energy cost from PV+BESS, amortized CapEx (compute & facility), cooling, network, staffing; compare to typical public-cloud list pricing for equivalent workloads.
  • Scheduling. Shift heavy training to solar peak; run inference/night ops from stored energy; curtail or defer as needed.
  • Carbon. On-site PV primary; grid as backup; report location-based and market-based emissions.

Figures on the site labeled "targeting" or "projected" reflect these assumptions and will be updated as real-world data accrues.
