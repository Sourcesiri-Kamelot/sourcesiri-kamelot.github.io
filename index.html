The Solution

TerraSolar Cerebra delivers compute, data, and energy through solar-aligned micro-data centers

✅ Targeting 50% Lower TCO via Solar

Targeting up to 50% lower total cost than typical hyperscaler stacks by aligning training to solar peaks and using DC-coupled storage. Methodology.

✅ Cloud-Compatible, Energy-Optimized

Integrates with public cloud where it makes sense; shifts batch workloads to our solar-aligned micro–data centers for cost and carbon advantages.

✅ Scalable Infrastructure

Modular solar + storage + compute units enable rapid deployment and scaling based on demand and solar resource availability.

✅ Energy-Aware Scheduling

Advanced ML models optimize workload placement based on solar generation forecasts, battery state, and energy costs.
